## 1. What RECOMBINATION actually means — the unified law (across all 9 lenses)

Nine disciplines, asked independently, converged on **one law in nine dialects**:

- **Category theory** (rec-math-cs): composition is a property of the **interface**, not the parts.
  Glue two systems by identifying their shared sub-object `Z` (a *pushout*); get `Z` right and the
  combination is *unique and automatic* (universal property). *"Don't ask how to make A talk to B — ask
  what shared object they're both defined over, build that, and the combination falls out."*
- **Art / montage** (rec-art): meaning lives in the **collision**, manufactured *in the receiver*
  crossing the gap. Don't blend to a seamless whole (mush) — **preserve the seam**. Koestler's
  *bisociation*: two complete unrelated frames, both true of one situation at once; the click IS the
  charge. *"Stop building the egregore directly — build the two matrices, engineer the seam, it's born
  in the receiver. That's what 'a shared vision that takes on life' mechanically IS."*
- **Design methods** (rec-design): forced combination is **subtractive, not additive** — enumerate the
  full space (Zwicky box), delete what the constraints forbid (cross-consistency), the invention is what
  *survives*. TRIZ: resolve a **contradiction** by separation (merge/nest/invert), don't compromise.
- **Science of science** (rec-sci-of-sci): the empirically-validated jaw-dropper = **conventional core
  + one atypical-tail element** (Uzzi Science 2013 — highest-impact papers pair a familiar core with an
  unusual combination). Most atypical combos fail; the *balance* is the recipe.
- **Biology** (rec-biology): **exaptation** — a trait evolved for one function co-opted for another;
  **symbiogenesis** — whole organisms merge into one (mitochondria); **the adjacent possible** — each
  combination opens new adjacent combinations.
- **Innovation econ** (rec-innovation): Weitzman *recombinant growth* — new ideas are recombinations of
  existing ideas, and the combinatorial explosion is the engine; value comes from **brokering across
  structural holes** (moving an idea from a domain where it's ordinary to one where it's revelatory).
- **Complexity** (rec-complexity): combination yields **emergence** (whole > parts) only under
  near-decomposability + shared coupling; autocatalytic closure is when the products catalyse each other.
- **Cognition** (rec-cognition): conceptual **blending** — two input spaces project to a blend with
  **emergent structure** present in neither; the magic is the emergent inference.
- **Analogy** (rec-analogy): **structure-mapping** — transfer the *relational structure* (not surface)
  from a source domain to a target; a mechanism from beast A becomes a new capability in beast B.

**The synthesis (the law):** a *magical* combination (vs a random mush) has three properties —
(1) a **shared interface / anchor** the parts genuinely meet on (pushout `Z`, montage's shared root,
boundary object); (2) a **preserved seam / distance** — the parts stay far apart and distinct so a
**third thing** can be born in the receiver (bisociation, atypical-tail, structural hole); (3) it
**removes a coupling / resolves a contradiction** rather than adding a layer (subtractive, exaptation
re-uses what exists). Too close = boring; too far with no anchor = noise; no seam = mush. Engineer the
*gap and the anchor*, not the pieces.

## 4. THE FLAGSHIP RECOMBINATION — "the Egregore Engine"

**Conventional core** (fundable, familiar): a grant-finding-and-writing service for a builder community.
**Atypical tail** (the jaw-drop): the applications are **not solicited from applicants — they are
detected by an embodied familiar that reads the community's public work and voices the latent
collaboration nobody had noticed yet.**

The loop, each edge an operator on two real sockets:

1. **Augur eyes → the graph.** `dig` + fuse enrich every member into a confidence-scored `Person` with
   their public-work footprint (repos, papers, projects). *(pushout: everything defined over the shared
   `Person` object.)*
2. **Cross-project rhyme (bisociation).** The specced-but-unbuilt "rhyme" engine runs over the enriched
   graph: find two members whose work *reaches for the same idea from opposite ends* — rarity-weighted,
   structural-gap-preferred (the serendipity math from `AUGUR-DESIGN.md` §8, and the belief-update deep
   cut). This produces an **Egregore candidate**: the third thing neither is building. *(montage: the
   seam is preserved — two distinct people, one shared root; the receiver crosses it.)*
3. **Dreamfinder voices it.** The familiar (one identity, many bodies) *performs* the rhyme — at an
   event on the room screen, or as a web/voice DM — "Maya writes climate grants, Tomás builds AR; the AR
   climate-storytelling proposal is sitting in the gap between you." *(TRIZ nest: each member's private DF
   nested in one shared egregore DF. Reader/Voice split: Claude finds the substance, the avatar performs it.)*
4. **Exapt the grant-writer.** If the egregore is fundable, the grant agents' **persuasion engine** —
   evolved to write applications — is *co-opted* to draft the collaboration's proposal against a real
   funder match. *(exaptation + structure-mapping.)*
5. **forge iterates it to a bar.** The draft goes through generate→adversarial-judge→iterate until it
   clears a quality/fundability threshold. *(generator-evaluator; the community as an autocatalytic set.)*
6. **Bridge-finder connects the humans + captures the outcome.** Warmest-path intro (Augur §7) puts the
   two people in a room; the **outcome** (did they collaborate? did it fund?) flows back to recalibrate
   the rhyme detector and the warmth graph. *(the closed loop — the one thing no incumbent has.)*
7. **Consolidation makes it learn.** The memory/resonance machinery consolidates each win into the
   community's own graph — the familiar remembers which rhymes became real.

**Why it's magnificent AND makes money:** it's Nick's literal mission (egregores = "shared visions that
take on life") made mechanical, *and* the money organ (grants) is the exapted engine — so the beautiful
thing and the revenue are the same motion. Nobody has "an embodied familiar that detects latent
collaborations across a real community's public work and writes the grant to fund them." It's
**design-for-subtraction at the community layer**: it removes the human task of *noticing who should
build together and finding the money*, which no human does at scale.

**Consent spine (non-negotiable, from AUGUR-DESIGN §2):** the rhyme is surfaced to the two people as an
*offer* ("Augur thinks your work rhymes — want the intro / the draft?"), never auto-acted. Inbound/
consented, provenance-shown. This is the same fault line as the avatar-quote funnel: the familiar
*invites*, it does not surveil.
